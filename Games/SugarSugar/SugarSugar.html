Sugar, Sugar

Description

Sugar, Sugar is a strategic puzzle game where you draw lines to direct sugar into cups. Build bridges and slopes to catch the sugar and guide it into the cup. Sugar, Sugar was developed by Bart Bonte and delivers a fresh challenge with each level.

Description by Crazy Games

Controls

Use mouse to draw lines to guide the sugar
